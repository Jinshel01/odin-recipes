Perfect Pumpkin Pie

The one and only! Odin Recipes makes this traditional dessert for the perfect ending to a Thanksgiving feast.

Detailed step and guide for this delicious traditional dessert

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin(15 ounce)
  • Condensed Milk(14 ounce)
  • Large Eggs(2)
  • Ground Cinnamon(1 tsp)
  • Ground ginger(0.5 tsp)
  • Ground Netmeg(0.5 tsp)
  • Salt(0.5 tsp)
  • 1 Unbaked Pie Crust(9 inch)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in medium
    bowl until smooth. Pour into crust. Bake 15 minutes.
  2. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F and continue baking 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted
    1 inch from crust comes out clean. Cool. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
