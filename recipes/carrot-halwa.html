Carrot Halwa

A delicious looking Carrot ka Halwa

A Carrot Halwa recipe with a detailed step-by-step guide

Gonna share the traditional way of making a delicious gajar ka halwa without any shortcuts or tweaks

Ingredients

  • Carrots(650g)
  • Full-fat Milk(4 cups)
  • Clarified Butter(4 tbsp)
  • Sugar(10-12 tbsp)
  • Cardamom Powder(0.5-1 tsp)
  • Nuts and Dry fruits(of your choice)
